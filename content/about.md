In early 2025, they offered me a Product Owner (PO) role, which I couldn't refuse.

Being in a  Product role feels like where I should be: at the intersection of business, tech, market and users. 
I focus on finding meaningful problems to solve and designing and validating solutions.

I’d love to continue growing and learning.
So, if you’re curious, eager to collaborate, or just want to chat, I’d love to connect.
