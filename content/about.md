Hi! I’m Matheus.

I’m glad you’re here. I built this site with care to share a little about myself.

Since secondary school, I’ve loved science and technology, which led me to study Chemical Engineering. That helped me develop an organised, analytical mindset for working in complex environments.

In 2021 I started working in the compliance area at [CTAIMA](https://ctaima.net/contractor-management/), a SaaS contractor management company.
There, after reporting many bugs (my best guess), they offered me a Software Tester (QA) position, which brought me a great deal of joy and learning. This is my start in the software products ecosystem.

Then, in 2023 I felt the need to learn other ways of building software products. So I got a position at [fundcraft](https://www.fundcraft.lu/about-us/), a Fintech company that's changing the game of regulated digital funds operations. And, in early 2025, they offered me a Product Owner (PO) role.

Being a PO feels like where I should be. This, at the intersection of business, tech, market and users. I focus on finding meaningful problems to solve, designing and validating solutions, and contributing to build the best product possible.

I’d love to continue growing and learning.

So, if you’re curious, eager to collaborate, or just want to chat, I’d love to connect.

Thanks for reading.

Matheus
