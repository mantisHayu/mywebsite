Hi — I’m Matheus.

I’m glad you’re here. I built this site with care to share a little about myself.

I’ve loved science and technology since secondary school, which led me to study Chemical Engineering. That helped me develop an organised, analytical mindset for working in complex environments.

In 2021 I was working in the compliance area at [CTAIMA](https://ctaima.net/contractor-management/), a SaaS contractor management, health & safety and ESG company.
There, after reporting many bugs (my best guess), they offered me a Software Tester position (QA), which brought me a great deal of joy and learning.

Then, in 2023 I felt the need to learn other ways of building software. So I got a QA position at [fundcraft](https://www.fundcraft.lu/about-us/), a digital fund operations company. And, in early 2025, they offered me a Product Owner role.

Being at the intersection of business, tech, the market and users makes me thrive — I focus on finding meaningful problems to solve, designing and validating solutions, and contributing to build the best product possible.

I’d love to continue growing and learning about organisational culture, product, software (APIs in particular), systems and design thinking.

If you’re curious, eager to collaborate, or just want to chat, I’d love to connect.

Thanks for reading.

Matheus
