Hi — I’m Matheus.

I’m glad you’re here. I’ve built this site with care to share a bit about me.

I’ve loved science and technology since high school, which led me to study Chemical Engineering. That helped me build an organised and analytical mindset for working in complex environments.

In 2021 I was working at a Health, Security, Environment and Quality company in the compliance area, where as I was opening many bug tickets, they offered me a Quality Tester position (QA), which I immediately accepted and which brought me a lot of joy, and learning.

Then, in 2023 I moved to a FinTech company as a QA and in early 2025 I was promoted to Product Owner. Being at the intersection of business, tech, the market and end users makes me thrive — I focus on finding meaningful problems, validating them, sharing insights, and contributing to the best solution at hand.

I’d love to keep growing and learning about organisational culture, product, software (APIs in particular), system and design thinking.

If you’re curious, eager to collaborate, or just want to chat, I’d love to connect.

Thanks for reading.
